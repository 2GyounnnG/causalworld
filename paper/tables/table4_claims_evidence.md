# Table 4. Claims, Evidence, and Boundaries

| manuscript claim | supporting evidence | key numbers | boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| The preflight protocol gives actionable prior recommendations. | Preflight benchmark summary and individual preflight reports. | Labels include no gain, temporal sufficient, candidate topology-specific, generic smoothing, and audit-supported positive cases. | Recommendations apply only under tested model and budget conditions. |
| Spectrum-matched permuted controls are necessary for topology attribution. | Master decision rules and graph specificity table. | HO lattice lambda=0.1 beats permuted at H32; many rMD17 cases do not. | A graph beating no-prior alone is not topology evidence. |
| Calibrated temporal smoothing changes graph-prior attribution. | Calibrated spring-mass, graph-wave, and n-body preflight reports. | Spring-mass temporal H32 `0.5689` vs graph `0.5740`; graph-wave temporal `0.5663` vs graph `0.5712`; n-body graph `0.5433` vs temporal `0.6453`. | Calibration is required because raw prior losses differ by scale. |
| Graph-prior gains can be low-budget-only. | Quick/standard comparisons for spring-mass, graph-wave, and n-body. | Spring-mass graph H32 quick `0.5740` vs standard `0.1048`, while standard no-prior is `0.0809`; graph-wave standard no-prior is best; n-body standard permuted is best. | Does not imply graph priors never help full-budget runs. |
| Candidate topology-specific usefulness appears in n-body quick mode. | `nbody_distance_ep5_temporal_calibrated` report. | Graph H32 `0.5433`, permuted `0.5838`, temporal `0.6453`, none `0.8216`. | This is not proof that the distance-kNN graph is the physical interaction graph. |
| Audit mode can support topology-aligned latent smoothing. | Cycle 8 checkpointed HO lattice audit. | Graph H32 `0.0169`, permuted `0.0198`, random `0.0199`; graph `D_true_norm(Delta H)` `2.8106` vs permuted `3.5109`; graph R_low K=4 `0.1866` vs permuted `0.1216`. | Controlled positive case; not universal latent-alignment evidence. |
| Raw graph-dynamics alignment is not sufficient. | Cycle 6 diagnostics, METR-LA, graph low-frequency lattice, graph heat. | METR raw true `D_dX_norm` `0.2380` vs permuted `0.3044`, but graph H32 is worse than no-prior; low-frequency lattice raw alignment favors true graph but quick permuted H32 is better. | Raw diagnostics are supporting probes, not decision rules. |
| Graph-generated dynamics does not guarantee graph-prior utility. | Graph heat lattice report and failure audit. | H32 none `0.6036`, graph `0.6902`, permuted `0.6058`. | The prior acts on learned latents, not directly on the simulator equation. |
